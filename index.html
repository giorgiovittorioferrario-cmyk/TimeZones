About this world time zone map

This interactive map shows the time zones of the world, including current local times, GMT and UTC offsets, timezone names, and daylight saving status. Use it to compare world times quickly and understand how different countries and regions are grouped by time zone.

Learn more about world time zones

A time zone map shows regions that share the same standard time. This map helps answer common searches such as world time zone map, current time around the world, GMT offset map, UTC offset by country, and daylight saving time map.

  • Check current local time in countries and regions
  • See official timezone names such as Europe/London or America/New_York
  • Compare GMT and UTC offsets across the world
  • See whether a location is using standard time or daylight saving time
